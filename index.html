CrochetPatternTemplate

Hello, my fellow crocheters.

I've created this template to make our lives easier when it comes down to writing patterns. It is designed to give you your pattern in pdf format. You can see below that it has some default fields, which represent information that most patterns include. They are not prefilled, information displayed is for demonstrational purposes only. If you don't care for them, just leave them blank and they won't be included in the final draft.

Custom fields are added with the following buttons:
  • adds a heading field to help you structure pattern with multiple parts;
  • adds a paragraph field for additional explanations, notes, descriptions, etc;
  • adds a field for image upload;
  • indicate whether you are working in rows or rounds, respectively and toggles between the two;
  • adds a field for next row/round;
  • resets row/round count.
Keep in mind that after resetting the count, you won't be able to go back to your old count.

After submitting the form you will be able to see a rough preview of your pdf pattern bellow, together with a download button.

This site does not grant me access to your pattern, but if you want to share it with me, or otherwise get in touch you can find me on instagram .
